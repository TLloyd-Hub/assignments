Who I Am

I was born on Vancouver Island, BC, Canada where I lived most of my life. I attended College in Victoria, BC where I earned an associate degree in business administration majoring in accounting. Most recently I was in living Grande Prairie, a small town in northern Alberta. I just moved to Seattle in March of this year to attend College at LW Tech.

What I've been doing

I recently got married in December 2025. Our wedding was in Campbell River, BC on Vancouver Island. For our honeymoon, we went to London, England where we were able to see many attractions like the London Eye, St. Paul's Cathedral, Tower Bridge, and The Shard (a 70+ story tower of glass overlooking London).

My Hobbies

When I am not at work or school I enjoy spending my time painting, cooking, gaming, and learning graphic design. I have made several paintings using acrylic paints and canvas. I have been cooking for many years, some of my favourite things to make are pizza, spaghetti, and tacos. I have used my graphic design and coding skills to create some mods for my favourite games that I have uploaded for others to enjoy.
